#Do’s
1.Maintain a professional and respectful profile
2.Think before posting any content
3.Verify information before sharing
4.Use privacy settings to protect personal data
5.Engage positively and respectfully with others


#Don’ts
1.Do not share personal or sensitive information
2.Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
3.Do not spread misinformation or fake news
4.Avoid using abusive or disrespectful language
5.Do not engage in online arguments or conflicts
